ABOUT US

Advancing National Security Through Indigenous Innovation instead of Engineering indigenous defence systems for next-generation warfare

MCI Defence Technologies Pvt Ltd

is a pioneering Indian defence technology company focused on the design, development, and integration of next-generation, AI-driven defence systems.

We specialise in multi-domain autonomous surveillance, counter-drone solutions, remotely operated Vehicles and underwater security platforms delivering mission-critical capabilities for naval, coastal, and strategic defence environments.

Our solutions are built with a clear focus on operational reliability, regulatory compliance, and future-ready architecture, aligned with the requirements of the Indian Navy, Indian Coast Guard, DRDO, and Ministry of Defence.
