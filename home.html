WHO ARE WE

MCI Defence Technologies Pvt Ltd is the Defence research and development arm of MCI Group,
established to address the growing need for indigenous, intelligent, and integrated defence solutions.

We operate at the intersection of advanced engineering, artificial intelligence, naval systems, and
defence compliance, delivering technologies that are both operationally effective and certifiable under
stringent military standards.

Our Products

MULTI-DOMAIN SOLUTIONS

Our growing portfolio of indigenous defence systems spans counter-drone, unmanned, weapon, and power solutions designed for
modern multi-domain operations. From AI-driven detection and electronic countermeasures to autonomous maritime and
underwater platforms, our products deliver layered protection across air, surface, and underwater environments

  • BRAHM
    KAVACH

    BRAHM‑KAVACH is India’s first all‑domain counter‑drone system, delivering AI‑driven 360° protection across air, surface, and underwater environments.

  • AUTONOMOUS SURVEILLANCE BOATS (ASBs)

    Autonomous Surveillance Boats provide persistent, AI‑enabled maritime monitoring with autonomous and remote operations for coastal, harbour, and offshore security.

  • REMOTELY OPERATED VEHICLES (ROVs)

    ROVs are intelligent underwater platforms designed for inspection, surveillance, and intervention in deep and hazardous marine environments.

  • REMOTELY OPERATED WEAPON SYSTEMS

    Remotely Operated Weapon Systems enable stabilized, high‑accuracy targeting and engagement while keeping operators protected from hostile environments.

  • GAS TURBINE GENERATORS

    Gas Turbine Generators deliver compact, high‑efficiency power for naval and defence platforms, ensuring reliable performance in extreme operating conditions.
